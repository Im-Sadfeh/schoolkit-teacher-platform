# Schoolkit Revolutionizes Computer Science Education for Public School Teachers

## Empowering Teachers in Lebanon to Deliver High-Quality Computer Science Lessons Independently with Real-Time AI Support

The SchoolKit Teacher Platform is now transforming the way public school teachers in Lebanon deliver Computer Science and AI education. Designed for educators in disadvantaged and refugee-serving schools, Schoolkit provides structured lesson plans, real-time AI assistance, and comprehensive classroom tools, enabling teachers to confidently conduct lessons without external support. This development marks a significant step towards scalable, quality education in underserved regions.

### The Problem

In Lebanon's public schools, teachers face immense challenges in delivering modern Computer Science education. Many educators work in high-pressure environments with limited training and resources, often serving disadvantaged and refugee communities. The reliance on external coaching and support from initiatives like CodeBrave has been essential, yet it creates a bottleneck to scalability and sustainability. Manual processes for tracking attendance and assessments further strain teachers' time and resources, leaving little room for lesson adaptation or in-class problem-solving.

### The Solution

Schoolkit addresses these challenges by offering a comprehensive platform that integrates lesson planning, AI-powered real-time support, and automated administrative tools. With Schoolkit, teachers can access structured lesson plans and materials tailored to their classroom contexts and receive immediate assistance from an AI chatbot for lesson adaptation and troubleshooting. This reduces preparation time and increases classroom confidence. Additionally, Schoolkit's attendance and assessment tracking tools provide valuable insights into student progress and teacher performance, ensuring consistent educational quality across schools.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is Schoolkit?**  
A: Schoolkit is a web and mobile platform designed to empower public school teachers in Lebanon to deliver high-quality Computer Science and AI education independently, with real-time AI support and comprehensive classroom tools.

**Q: Who is Schoolkit for?**  
A: Schoolkit is for public school teachers in under-resourced schools in Lebanon, particularly those serving disadvantaged and refugee communities. These teachers typically have limited training in Computer Science but are motivated to provide engaging lessons.

**Q: What makes Schoolkit different from existing solutions?**  
A: Unlike static lesson plans and ad hoc support, Schoolkit provides real-time AI assistance, lesson adaptation tools, and integrated monitoring features, enabling independent, consistent, and confident delivery of Computer Science education.
